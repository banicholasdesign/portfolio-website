About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer based out of central Illinois. From a young age I always knew I wanted to do something in design. I remember sitting inside of Adobe Photoshop coming up with fun designs since the age of 12. I use to design cover photos, album artwork, and send spoof pictures to friends all in the matter of a 2 hour sit-down session at my computer. I was hooked.

Fast forward to present day when I stumbled upon digital design and I couldn't get enough! I started doing research on what UX/UI design was and I knew right away this was the path I had been searching for. All the pieces seemed to fall into place.

Interested in going back to school, I found CareerFoundry online and immediately applied not knowing if I would be accepted. To my surprise I had gotten an email the very next day, I had been approved. Relief and excitement set in and overcame my emotions. I was going to train to become a designer!

It is now my dream to start a design agency someday that caters to the needs of clients and their product visions. Studying UX, UI, and Front-end Development has really allowed me to hone in on my design skills. I consider myself a great team player with a knack for leadership as well.
